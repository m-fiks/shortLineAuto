About Us:

Short Line Auto, Inc. is a family-owned business that was established in Rochester, MN in 2002. It is owned by Jerry and Kim Rishavy. Their son, Jordan manages the business full-time. The family works hard to ensure the lot is always stocked with valuable cars!

They sell pre-owned cars, trucks and sport utility vehicles to both new customers and returning buyers. The Rishavys will demonstrate all of the capabilities of the vehicle to prospective buyers. Come on in!
